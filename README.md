# MoneyMove: Wealth Path Simulator

A free, interactive financial planning tool showing the long-term impact
of three different saving and investing strategies — using the same monthly
amount across all three paths.

Built by [Dorcas Aina](https://dorcasainaa-dotcom.github.io) — Analytics
Engineer | ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Lead Auditor.

**Live app:** [moneymoves.streamlit.app](https://moneymoves.streamlit.app)

---

## What It Does

Most people keep their money in a savings account and assume that is enough.
This tool shows — with your own numbers — exactly what that decision costs
over 20, 30, or 40 years compared to smarter alternatives.

Enter your salary, monthly spending, and how much you want to invest. The
simulator instantly calculates three parallel futures:

| Path | Strategy | Assumed Return |
|---|---|---|
| Path 1 | Traditional savings account | 4.5% per year |
| Path 2 | Stocks & Shares ISA — global index fund | 7.0% per year |
| Path 3 | Optimised: emergency fund, ISA, diversified portfolio | 8.0% per year |

All three paths use the same monthly investment amount. The difference is
not how much you save — it is where you put it.

---

## Features

- **Three-path wealth projection** — year by year up to 45 years
- **Work backwards from your goal** — set a target pot and see exactly
  what monthly investment each path requires
- **Emergency fund check** — calculates your personalised target and
  tracks progress
- **What if I saved more?** — four styled cards showing the compounding
  impact of small increases
- **Return assumptions explainer** — plain-English explanation of every
  return rate with customisable sliders
- **Retirement income calculator** — what your pot actually means in
  monthly income using the 3.5% sustainable withdrawal rate
- **Smart Money Moves guide** — four actionable steps with checklists
